Bambuina bambui: a new genus and species
of cave cricket from Brazil
(Orthoptera: Grylloidea:
Phalangopsidae: Luzarinae)

Bambuina bambui, a new genus and species of phalangopsid cricket,
is described from Gruta do Centenário, a quartzite
cave located in Serra do Inficionado (Inficionado Ridge), a subset
of mountains belonging to the Serra do Caraça (Caraça
Ridge) complex in the State of Minas Gerais, Brazil.
